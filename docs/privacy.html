Privacy Policy for SmartScan AI

Last updated: March 8, 2026

SmartScan AI ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we process, why we process it, and what choices you have.

1. Data We Process

  • Images you choose to scan from your device gallery.
  • Extracted text and metadata generated by OCR.
  • Subscription and billing state received from Google Play Billing.
  • Device advertising identifier (for ad personalization via Google AdMob).
  • Basic technical diagnostics (crash and app performance logs, if enabled).

2. How We Use Data

  • To scan images and extract text (all processing happens locally on your device).
  • To provide search, sorting, and sync features.
  • To verify premium access and restore purchases.
  • To serve personalized ads in the free tier (via Google AdMob).
  • To improve app quality and stability.

Important: We do not upload your photos or scanned text to our servers. All image processing and text extraction happens locally on your device. Your data remains private and under your control.

3. Billing and Payments

Payments are processed by Google Play. We do not collect or store full payment card details. Purchase status is handled through Google Play Billing APIs. Premium purchases remove ads and unlock unlimited scanning.

4. Third-Party Services (Advertising)

SmartScan AI uses Google AdMob to display ads in the free tier. AdMob may collect device identifiers (advertising ID), IP address, and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Learn more about Google's advertising practices: Google Ads Policy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with service providers only as needed to operate the app:

  • Google Play Services: for billing and subscription management.
  • Google AdMob: for serving ads in the free tier (advertising ID and usage data).

6. Data Retention

Scanned content remains on your device/app storage until you delete it. Billing records are retained according to Google Play policies. Advertising identifiers are managed by Google AdMob and retained according to their policies.

7. Your Choices

  • You can revoke media permissions in Android settings.
  • You can delete scans in the app at any time.
  • You can cancel subscriptions in Google Play.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • You can reset your advertising ID: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.
