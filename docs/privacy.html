Privacy Policy for SmartScan AI

Last updated: 2026-03-08

SmartScan AI ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we process, why we process it, and what choices you have.

1. Data We Process

  • Images you choose to scan from your device gallery.
  • Extracted text and metadata generated by OCR.
  • Subscription and billing state received from Google Play Billing.
  • Basic technical diagnostics (crash and app performance logs, if enabled).

2. How We Use Data

  • To scan images and extract text.
  • To provide search, sorting, and sync features.
  • To verify premium access and restore purchases.
  • To improve app quality and stability.

3. Billing and Payments

Payments are processed by Google Play. We do not collect or store full payment card details. Purchase status is handled through Google Play Billing APIs.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with service providers only as needed to operate the app (for example, Google Play services for billing).

5. Data Retention

Scanned content remains on your device/app storage until you delete it. Billing records are retained according to Google Play policies.

6. Your Choices

  • You can revoke media permissions in Android settings.
  • You can delete scans in the app at any time.
  • You can cancel subscriptions in Google Play.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: kukliskirill@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.
